# Metro Cardz — Loyalty System & Marketing Guide
*A complete playbook for merchants to operate, scale, and drive customer retention.*

---

## 📱 System Overview

Metro Cardz is an industry-grade loyalty platform that bridges physical NFC/QR membership cards with a digital customer engagement suite. The platform operates on two core portals:
1. **Merchant Portal**: For store owners and staff to register customers, log visits, issue rewards, and monitor campaign performance.
2. **Customer Portal (Public)**: A mobile-friendly pass showing the customer their loyalty points balance, active coupons, unlockable rewards, scratch cards, and a direct referral link to invite friends.

---

## 🔄 The 4-Step Operational Flow (Day-to-Day)

### Step 1: Enroll a New Member
When a new customer arrives at your store:
1. Open the **Merchant Dashboard** and go to **Add Member**.
2. Enter their name and phone number.
3. Select their membership tier (e.g. Silver, Gold, Platinum).
4. **Marketing Tip:** If they were referred by a friend, enter their friend's **Referral Code**. This automatically credits the friend with a referral bonus (configurable in Settings, default: 50 points) and triggers a message to the friend.

### Step 2: Link a Physical Card
To issue a physical card with a unique serial number / QR:
1. Navigate to **Card Inventory**.
2. Click **Link Card** on any available card.
3. Type or scan the customer's card number or QR.
4. Select the member you enrolled in Step 1. The physical card is now locked to their digital account. 
5. When the customer scans the card QR in the future, it automatically routes them to their **Public Member Profile** displaying their points and offers.

### Step 3: Log a Visit & Record Sales
When a member visits your store:
1. Search for the member by Name, Phone, or scan their physical card.
2. Click **Record Visit**.
3. Enter the total **Bill Amount** (e.g. ₹1,200).
4. Select any discount **Coupon** the customer wants to use (this validates dates and usage count).
5. Click **Confirm**. The system will:
   - Log the visit and increment their visit count.
   - Award points based on your active **Points Rules** (e.g., flat points or points per rupee spent).
   - Log an immutable audit trail in the **Loyalty History**.
   - Auto-issue milestone rewards if they cross visit/spend thresholds.

### Step 4: Claim Rewards
When a customer wants to redeem their points:
1. Go to their profile under **Search Member**.
2. Click the **Claim Reward** tab.
3. Select the reward from your catalog (e.g., "Free Hair Wash - 500 Points").
4. Click **Claim**. The system checks their balance, deducts the points, and updates the stock count.

## 📣 Marketing & Growth Playbook

Use these built-in engines to turn one-time buyers into lifelong advocates.

### 1. The Referral Virality Engine (WhatsApp Referrals)
Customers are your best marketers. Metro Cardz lets them share invitations instantly:
- **How it works**: Every customer has a unique 8-character referral code on their public pass.
- **The Share Loop**: The customer clicks the "Share on WhatsApp" button on their phone. This opens WhatsApp with a pre-written message containing their referral link.
- **The Reward**: When their friend signs up using that link, the original customer is automatically credited with referral points, and they receive an SMS alert: *"Thanks for referring Raj! 50 points have been added to your card."*

### 2. Points Earning Rules (Strategic Incentives)
Configure your earning structure in **Rewards → Points Rules**:
- **Rule Type: Per Visit**: Give customers a flat points bonus on every transaction (e.g., 20 points per visit). This drives **visit frequency** regardless of ticket size.
- **Rule Type: Per Rupee**: Award points proportional to spending (e.g., 1 point per ₹10 spent). This incentivizes **higher average order value (AOV)**.
- *Best Practice:* Run double-points weekends or holiday campaigns by updating rules to boost sales during slow seasons.

### 3. Automated Reminder Campaigns (Automated Messaging)
Configure automated alerts in **Campaigns → Rules** that run in the background. The system sweeps your database hourly to notify members based on triggers:
- **Birthday Campaign**: Send an automatic greeting and a unique coupon code X days before their birthday (e.g., *"Happy Birthday week! Show this message for a free dessert."*)
- **Anniversary Campaign**: Reward loyalty on the anniversary of their enrollment.
- **Expiry Reminder**: Prevent churn by alerting customers when their membership is expiring (e.g., *"Your Gold status expires in 7 days! Visit us to keep your benefits."*)
- **Threshold Alerts**: Push customers who are close to key reward milestones (e.g., *"You have 450 points! Only 50 more to unlock a free coffee."*)

### 4. Gamification (Scratch Cards & Lucky Draws)
Drive engagement by making loyalty fun:
- **Milestone Scratch Cards**: Automatically award digital scratch-and-win cards on specific visits (e.g. 5th visit, 10th visit). Customers rub the card screen to reveal instant points or gift vouchers.
- **Lucky Draws**: Run monthly raffles. Set entry criteria (e.g., *"Must have 300 points and 5 visits to enter"*). The system randomly draws a winner and updates the status to "Drawn," notifying the lucky customer.

## ⚙️ Settings & Configuration Checklist

To ensure your system is fully optimized:
1. Go to **Settings** and upload your business logo. This appears on all customer-facing profiles and public views.
2. Verify that your **WhatsApp Number** is correctly set so notifications match your brand.
3. Configure the default **Referral Bonus** points value.
4. Establish at least 3 high-value items in your **Reward Catalog** to entice new signups.
