Frequently Asked Questions

Do customers need to download an app?+

No. Customers receive a unique card link by WhatsApp or SMS. They bookmark it — it works like an app on any smartphone browser without any install.

How do staff scan cards?+

Staff use the Metro Cardz merchant portal (works on any device with a camera). They can scan the QR code or simply search by name / phone number.

How long does card printing take?+

Standard PVC cards ship within 3–5 working days. Premium gold foil and holographic cards take 5–7 working days. Minimum order is 100 cards.

Can I manage multiple locations?+

Yes. You can add unlimited staff accounts per location, each with scoped access. The platform supports multi-outlet operation out of the box.

What if a customer loses their card?+

No problem. Each member has a permanent digital card link that works independent of the physical card. The QR can also be re-issued.

Is there a contract or lock-in?+

Metro Cardz is month-to-month — cancel any time. Annual plans get a 20% discount. No lock-in, no setup fees.
